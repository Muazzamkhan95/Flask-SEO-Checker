🚀 Welcome to the SEO Checker App

This web app helps you analyze and track the SEO performance of any website. Powered by Flask, PostgreSQL, and BeautifulSoup, it lets you:

  • 🔍 Enter any website URL and get instant SEO analysis
  • 📊 View structured SEO scores based on metadata, tags, and structure
  • 🧠 Track your past analysis with user-based SEO history
  • 📈 Visualize trends in SEO score with interactive charts

Whether you're an agency, freelancer, or just curious — this tool gives you a simple yet powerful dashboard to monitor SEO health.
